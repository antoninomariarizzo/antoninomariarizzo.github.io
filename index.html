Bio

I am completing my Ph.D. in Artificial Intelligence at Politecnico di Torino, specializing in Deep Learning, Computer Vision, and Signal Processing. My research on advanced monitoring techniques for fault detection, in collaboration with Cisco Photonics, has resulted in publications and two international patents. I previous received an MSc in Computer Science and Engineering from Politecnico di Milano.

Retrieval-Augmented Generation with PDF files and OCR

Python, PyTorch, Hugging Face Transformers, FAISS, PyMuPDF, Tesseract OCR
