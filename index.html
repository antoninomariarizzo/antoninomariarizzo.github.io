Bio

Antonino Maria Rizzo was born in Mazara del Vallo (TP) on November 22nd, 1995. He received the Laurea magistrale (M.Sc.) in Computer Science and Engineering from the Politecnico di Milano (IT) in 2021. He is currently pursuing the National PhD in Artificial Intelligence - Industry 4.0 at the Politecnico di Milano. He is involved in an industrial research project with Cisco Photonics for fiber optics monitoring.
